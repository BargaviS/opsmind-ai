COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT

Leave Policy:
- Annual Leave: Every employee is entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Sick Leave: Employees get 12 days of paid sick leave per year.
- Maternity Leave: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Paternity Leave: Male employees get 5 days of paid paternity leave.
- Casual Leave: 6 days per year for personal emergencies.

Work Hours:
- Standard working hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Saturday is optional work from home day.
- Overtime will be compensated with additional pay or comp-off.

Salary and Benefits:
- Salaries are credited on the last working day of every month.
- Employees get health insurance covering self, spouse, and 2 children.
- Annual performance bonus based on rating: Excellent=20%, Good=15%, Average=10%.
- Provident Fund contribution of 12% from both employee and employer.

Remote Work Policy:
- Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week.
- Must be available on video call during core hours 10 AM to 4 PM.
- Remote work not allowed during probation period of 6 months.

Resignation Policy:
- Notice period is 30 days for junior staff and 60 days for senior staff.
- Full and final settlement within 45 days of last working day.
- Earned leaves will be encashed during full and final settlement.

Code of Conduct:
- Employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
- Harassment of any kind will result in immediate termination.
- Confidential company information must not be shared outside.
